short description:
The Sieve of Eratosthenes is an ancient, highly efficient algorithm used to find all prime numbers up to a specified
integer . It works by creating a list from 2 to n, then iteratively marking the multiples of each prime number (starting with 2) as composite (not prime). It runs in time.
